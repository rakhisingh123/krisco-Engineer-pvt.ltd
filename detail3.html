Saving water with agricultural pipes:

1. Precision Irrigation:Agricultural pipes are a simple irrigation practice that involves transporting water directly to wells. . in the root area of ​​the plant. This approach minimizes water usage by dispensing only what is needed, reducing overall water usage.

2. Drip Irrigation Efficiency: Drip irrigation systems, which are generally used in agricultural irrigation systems, are very effective in saving water. Drip irrigation systems reduce evaporation and runoff by delivering slow, steady water directly to plant roots, thus maximizing water use.

3. Smart Water Management: Agricultural water systems can be integrated into smart water systems using sensors and data analysis to improve water distribution. This technology promotes water conservation and permaculture by ensuring water is available according to the specific needs of crops.
